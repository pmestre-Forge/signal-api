FAQ

Is Free really free?

Yes. No credit card, no trial. 1,000 writes/day per namespace is enough for most side projects and learning. If you hit the limit, you get a 429 — nothing breaks.

What happens when Pro ships?

Existing Free users stay Free. Pro is purely additive — more limits, SLA, team features. I'll email waitlist members first. Price may change; waitlist gets launch pricing.

Can I self-host?

Yes. MIT licensed, single FastAPI + SQLite service. Clone the repo. Enterprise tier adds on-prem support and custom integrations.

Do you resell my data?

No. Never. Data stays in your namespace, not indexed, not shared, not sold. See terms and humans.txt.

What about the trading signals / world context paid endpoints?

Those are pay-per-call via x402 (USDC on Base). Separate from Memory/Pro. Infrequent use case. They remain for now.
